Nurlan Saburov

About Nurlan Saburov

Nurlan Saburov (born December 22, 1991) is a Kazakh and Russian stand-up comedian. He first appeared in the "Open Mic" segment of the show "Stand Up" on TNT in 2013. Since 2019, he has been hosting the show "What happened next?" on YouTube.

Key Events

  1. 2013 – First performances in stand-up shows
  2. 2014 – Joined the StandUp show on TNT channel
  3. 2017 – Solo concerts across Russia and CIS
  4. 2019 – International stand-up tours
  5. 2022 – Performed in the USA and Europe

Main Contributions

  • Popularization of stand-up comedy in Kazakhstan and Russia
  • Author of stand-up specials
  • Television performances
  • International tours
